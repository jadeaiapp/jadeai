4. Purposes & Legal Bases

We process personal data for the purposes and legal grounds below:

  • Provide and operate the Service: authenticate users, generate and store content, manage templates and credits (Contract performance).
  • Improve functionality: analytics, debugging, testing, and developing new features (Legitimate interest).
  • Security and fraud prevention: detect abuse, enforce policies, protect user accounts (Legitimate interest / Legal obligation).
  • Customer support: respond to help requests, provide notifications, and maintain communication (Contract performance).
  • Billing and compliance: manage subscriptions, fulfil tax and regulatory requirements (Legal obligation).
  • Marketing and announcements (optional): send updates when you opt in (Consent). You can withdraw consent at any time.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active. Upon deletion, we keep limited records for up to 180 days to comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse, after which data is deleted or anonymized.
  • Generated content: stored until you delete it or request removal.
  • Analytics & diagnostics: anonymized after 90 days.
  • Support tickets: retained for up to 24 months to resolve issues and meet audit requirements.

6. Data Sharing & International Transfers

We share personal data with service providers that enable the Service. These processors act under our instructions and implement security measures.

  • Google Firebase: authentication, Firestore database, analytics, crash reporting, and cloud storage (servers located in the EU and/or US).
  • Google Gemini API: content generation requests (processed on Google’s infrastructure).
  • Google Play / Apple App Store: subscription and payment processing.
  • Analytics & monitoring: Firebase Analytics, Remote Config, and similar tools to understand usage patterns.

When transferring data outside Türkiye or the European Economic Area, we rely on adequate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses and encrypted channels. By using the Service, you understand that your data may be processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your home jurisdiction.
